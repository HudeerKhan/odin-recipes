Smash Burgers

Description

A smash burger is a type of hamburger made by pressing a ball of ground beef onto a hot griddle or skillet, creating a crispy, caramelized crust on the outside while keeping the inside juicy. The technique results in a burger with a rich, flavorful crust and a juicy interior, often topped with classic ingredients like cheese, lettuce, tomato, and pickles.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (80/20 blend of meat to fat ratio)
  • Potato roll
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese slice
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Butter/oil for toasting buns
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:

    2. Gather all your ingredients: ground beef, burger buns, cheese slices (if using), lettuce, pickles, etc.

  2. Preheat Your Cooking Surface:

    3. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium-high heat. If using a cast-iron skillet, make sure it's well-heated for a good sear.

  3. Shape the Beef:

    4. Form the ground beef into loose balls, about 4 to 6 ounces each. Don’t overwork the meat; it should be loosely packed.

  4. Toast the Buns:

    5. While the skillet heats up, butter the insides of the burger buns and toast them in a separate pan or on a griddle until golden brown. Set aside.

  5. Cook the Patties:

    6. Once the skillet is hot, place a beef ball onto the surface and use a spatula to press it down firmly, creating a thin patty. Press hard enough to make contact with the pan but don’t squish it too much.

  6. Season the Patties:

    7. Season the top of the patty with salt and pepper while it cooks.

  7. Cook the Patties:

    8. Let the patty cook without moving it for about 2-3 minutes, allowing a crispy crust to form. Flip the patty and immediately place a slice of cheese on top if using. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the patty reaches your desired level of doneness.

  8. Assemble the Burgers:

    9. Place the cooked patty on the bottom half of the toasted bun. Add your desired toppings: lettuce, tomato slices, pickles, onions, ketchup, and mustard.

  9. Top and Serve:

    10. Place the top half of the bun over the toppings. Serve immediately while hot and juicy.

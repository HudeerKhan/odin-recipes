Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy pasta dish featuring fettuccine noodles coated in a rich, velvety Alfredo sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese. Tender pieces of cooked chicken are added to the pasta and sauce, creating a comforting and indulgent meal. The dish is known for its smooth, savory flavors and is often garnished with fresh parsley for a touch of color.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the Chicken:

    2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the chicken for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked and golden brown. Remove from the skillet, let rest for a few minutes, then slice into strips or cubes.

  2. Cook the Pasta:

    3. While the chicken is cooking, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. Make the Alfredo Sauce:

    4. In the same skillet used for the chicken, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, until fragrant.

    Pour in the heavy cream and chicken broth. Bring to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly.

  4. Add Cheese:

    5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season with additional salt and pepper if needed.

  5. Combine Pasta and Chicken:

    6. Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce, tossing to coat the pasta evenly. Add the sliced or cubed chicken on top and stir to combine.

  6. Serve:

    7. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired. Serve the Chicken Alfredo hot.
